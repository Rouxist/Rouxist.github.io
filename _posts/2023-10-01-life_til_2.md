### Importance Sampling

$$
\begin{aligned}
E_{p}[f(x)] & = \int p(x)f(x)dx \\
& = \int \frac{q(x)}{q(x)}p(x)f(x)dx \\ 
& = \int q(x)[\frac{p(x)}{q(x)}f(x)]dx \\ 
& = E_{q}[\frac{p(x)}{q(x)}f(x)] \\
\end{aligned}
$$
